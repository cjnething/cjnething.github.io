Bio
Jeanette has always had a passion for the beauty industry. Oregon native, she ventured to San Diego and completed her training at the artful Paul Mitchell Academy in 2008. Inspired by fashion, music, art, and the latest beauty trends, she set her sight on Los Angeles. While in LA she has had tremendous opportunities from working with many creative teams on editorial shoots, exclusive training by elite Redken artist, and working at prestigious salons such as Umberto and Meche. In her free time she enjoys travel, food, and learning new things. She specializes in sun-kissed blondes and beachy waves.
