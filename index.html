About

I'm a researcher and builder interested in the intersection of product design, learning sciences, and AI. I care about experiences that make people progressively more capable, curious, and empowered—integrating skill development into product design rather than treating it as an afterthought.

My work spans rapid prototyping, user research, and production engineering. I'm skeptical of tutorials and onboarding flows that optimize for engagement; I'd rather measure whether someone can actually do something new afterward.

  • Full-stack prototyping with strong front-end craft
  • HCI, skill acquisition, and capability-focused metrics
  • Translating research insights into shipped product
